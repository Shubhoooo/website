Computer Safety And Security

Computer safety refer to the protection of computer-based resources against unauthorised use or physical damage.

You know that computers are so expensive, so if you have computer,you must take care of it. It is not difficult to keep your computer healthy and in good working condition. Maintaining a computer involve three things:

• Keeping it physically clean

• Protecting it malware

• Backing up your important files
